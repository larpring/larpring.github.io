larpring

an invite-only loop of sites people made by hand.

how it works

every member's site links to the next one. hit next and you land on someone else's page; keep going and the chain eventually curls back to where you started. a circle of sites wired together by hand, with no algorithm deciding the order and no feed in the middle.

give it a spin: prev random next

why though

because the best parts of the web were always the pages people built just because they wanted to. no metrics, no ads, nobody optimizing for your time. a ring is an old, honest way to wander between those pages and find the next one. the longer rant lives in the manifesto.
